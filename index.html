🧭 How to Use TabFin

Follow this guide step by step on your first setup, then refer back anytime.

📲 Install TabFin as an App

TabFin works as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — you can install it on your phone or desktop for a full app experience: home screen icon, full screen (no browser bar), and works completely offline.

🤖 Android (Chrome)
Chrome shows an automatic "Install app" banner at the bottom. Tap it to install TabFin directly to your home screen.
🍎 iPhone (Safari)
Tap the Share ↑ button, then tap "Add to Home Screen". TabFin opens full screen like a native app.
✅ Once installed — data still auto-saves in your browser. Works offline. No account needed.
⚠️ Installing on a second device does not sync your data — use Export/Import to transfer data between devices.
